Data Definitions & Assumptions

Standard Credit (The Remainder)

This accounts for all credit card volume that is NOT Amex and NOT High-Premium Rewards.

  • Examples: Standard Visa/Mastercard, Debit-like Credit.
  • Assumption: Modeled at a baseline cost of 2.50%.
  • Calculation: Total Volume - Debit - Amex - Premium Rewards.

Premium Rewards Intensity

How many customers use high-perk cards (Chase Sapphire, etc.)?

  • The Cost: These cards cost significantly more (Interchange ~3.30%+).

Debit Mix (Regulated + Unregulated)

Percentage of volume processed via Debit cards.

  • Assumption: Modeled at a weighted cost of 1.50%.
